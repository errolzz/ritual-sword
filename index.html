Movement:

Tap a tile adjacent to Brinley to move there.

Tiles with red items cannot be moved through.

Sword Attack:

Tapping a monster adjacent to Brinley will attack it with his sword.

Shield and 360 Attack:

Hold down on Brinley to activate his shield.

Release his shield for a special 360 degree sword attack.

The special attack gets stronger the longer the shield is active.

Bow Attack:

Flick the screen starting at Brinley to fire an arrow.

Arrows cannot be fired if an enemy is next to Brinley.

Status Bar:

The sword and arrow icons tell you when your attack skills are ready.

The red bar displays how much life you have before you are killed.
